Remote Support Portal

Complete the 3 steps below to allow a technician to help you with your Android device.

1 Get QuickSupport App

Install the official TeamViewer QuickSupport app from the Google Play Store.

Get it on Google Play
2 Open App & Grant Permissions

Open QuickSupport. Android will ask you to enable special permissions. This is mandatory for screen sharing.

1. Find "QuickSupport" in the list.

Settings
QuickSupport
Set to ON

2. Also allow Accessibility and any required Add-ons if prompted.

3 Send ID to Technician

Look at the TeamViewer app. Once permissions are set, a 9 or 10-digit ID will appear. Type it below:

Then, send it to the technician via WhatsApp.
